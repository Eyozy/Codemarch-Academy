Learn HTML, CSS, Tailwind & Git

Become a Developer with Practical Experience in 30 Days

"30 Days CodeCamp" is a 30-day programming learning event designed for coding enthusiasts. Its goal is to help learners quickly master front-end development technologies such as HTML, CSS, Tailwind, and Git, enabling them to independently complete projects.

More than 1,000+ people have joined our community

Course Features

At 30 Days CodeCamp, we offer a unique and comprehensive learning experience with a variety of course features designed to support and enrich your coding journey.

Free Tech Support:

We provide free technical support to all our students. Our experienced developers are always ready to assist with any technical challenges you may encounter.

Daily Assignments:

Our daily assignments are designed to reinforce your understanding and application of the course material, providing you with ample practice every day.

Build 10+ Projects:

Our courses include the creation of over 10 projects, allowing you to apply the skills you've learned in a practical setting.

Weekly Blogs:

We provide weekly blogs that cover various coding topics, keeping you updated with the latest trends and best practices in the industry.

Personal Branding Guide:

We offer a personal branding guide to help you build a strong online presence and showcase your skills to potential employers.

Learning Resources:

We provide a wealth of learning resources to supplement your learning, including tutorials, articles, and reference guides.

Content Creation Guide:

Our content creation guide provides tips and strategies for creating engaging and informative content, helping you to share your knowledge with the community.

Early Bird Offers from Codemarch:

As a student of 30 Days CodeCamp, you'll have access to early bird offers from Codemarch, giving you the opportunity to take advantage of our services at discounted rates.
